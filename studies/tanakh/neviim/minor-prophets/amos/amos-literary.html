Strategic Placement: The Amaziah Confrontation

The narrative of Amaziah's confrontation with Amos (7:10-17) is not randomly placed. It interrupts the vision sequence at a crucial momentâ€”immediately after the plumb line vision mentions "the house of Jeroboam."
