Macro Structure: From Emptiness to Fullness

Ruth forms a balanced four-part narrative with mirrored bookends (chapters 1 ‚Üî 4) and a transformative hinge in chapter 3. The structure moves deliberately from exile to homecoming, from death to life, from emptiness to abundance.

Legal Mechanics: Levirate vs. Redeemer Laws

Literary & Theological Significance

The chiasm centers on Ruth's courageous request at the threshing floor, where she invokes Boaz's own blessing about divine wings. This pivotal moment transforms the narrative from survival to redemption. Ruth moves from being one who seeks refuge to one who actively participates in redemption.
