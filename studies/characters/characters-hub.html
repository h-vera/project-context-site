Featured Character Profiles

Recently added and popular character studies

Abraham אַבְרָהָם

Father of faith, covenant recipient, and patriarch of Israel. His journey from Ur to Canaan exemplifies faith and obedience.

Genesis 11-25 • Multi-page Study
Sarah שָׂרָה

Mother of nations, wife of Abraham. Her story demonstrates faith through struggle with infertility and God's promise fulfillment.

Genesis 11-25 • Complete Profile
Moses מֹשֶׁה

Prophet, lawgiver, and deliverer of Israel from Egypt. Central figure in the Exodus and covenant at Sinai.

Exodus-Deuteronomy • Multi-page Study
Deborah דְּבוֹרָה

Prophet, judge, and military leader. The only female judge who led Israel to victory over Canaanite oppression.

Judges 4-5 • Multi-page Study
Hagar הָגָר

Egyptian servant who became mother of Ishmael. Her encounters with God reveal His compassion for the marginalized.

Genesis 16, 21 • Complete Profile
Delilah דְּלִילָה

Philistine woman who betrayed Samson. Her story explores themes of deception, weakness, and divine sovereignty.

Judges 16 • Complete Profile
