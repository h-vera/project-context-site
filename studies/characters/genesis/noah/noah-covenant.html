The phrase "God remembered" (Gen 8:1) doesn't mean God forgot. In Hebrew, zakar marks the moment when God acts on what He promised. From the human perspective, waiting feels like being forgotten. But "remembering" is the pivot pointâ€”the moment everything changes.
