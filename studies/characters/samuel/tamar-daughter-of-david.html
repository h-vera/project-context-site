Note on Hermeneutical Approach:

This profile prioritizes interpretive methods that distinguish between description and prescription, original meaning and contemporary application. The sensitive nature of sexual violence requires particular care to avoid re-traumatizing victims through careless interpretation or application.

Methodological Transparency:

Interpretive decisions are made explicit throughout, acknowledging where scholars disagree and why certain readings are preferred. The three-step hermeneutical bridge ensures applications are grounded in the text's original meaning rather than imposed upon it.
