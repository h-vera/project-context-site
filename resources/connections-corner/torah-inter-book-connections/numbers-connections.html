▸
Deuteronomy is Moses' answer to the question Numbers raises: can this people ever enter the land? The failures at Kadesh, Meribah, and Peor are retold as warnings. The census of the second generation replaces the first. Moses, barred from entry himself, pours everything into preparing the children for what the parents forfeited.
