Leviticus → Deuteronomy
10 connections
▸
Deuteronomy translates Levitical holiness for life in the land. Priestly cultic law becomes covenantal ethics. Worship is centralized. The blessings and curses of Leviticus 26 are expanded into Deuteronomy 28 — the stakes are restated for a new generation on the threshold of Canaan.
