יְהוָה יְהוָה אֵל רַחוּם וְחַנּוּן

One divine self-disclosure at Sinai — twelve major reuse points across the Tanakh, from Moses at Kadesh Barnea to Nehemiah's post-exilic prayer five centuries later. Widely regarded as the most re-quoted OT passage within the OT itself. The irony at the center: two prophets write about the same city, quote the same verse, and choose opposite halves.

The Source — Exodus 34:6–7
