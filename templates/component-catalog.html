🎓 Academic Standards
Where interpretive debates exist, multiple scholarly positions are presented with their supporting arguments. Avoid treating debated passages as settled.
Card · Posture

Academic Note Box

Plum-tinted bordered callout for academic-posture statements: how the study handles disputed readings, where it draws lines, what level of certainty it claims. Visually distinct from Methodology Card (which describes method) and from Critical Distinction (which corrects a specific misreading).

Use for: hermeneutical posture statements at the top of seminary-level studies — "we use this text edition," "we treat debated passages this way," "we cite scholarly positions even when we don't endorse them." One per page max; otherwise it stops feeling like a posture and starts feeling like hand-wringing.
